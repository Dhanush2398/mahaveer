MAHAVEER SCHEME

Empowering Women Through Financial Inclusion

and Sustainable Entrepreneurship

Scheme Details

Loans for micro and small business entities engaged in Trading, Manufacturing, Services, and allied Agriculture sectors like poultry, bee-keeping, and pisciculture.

01

Eligible Borrowers

Individuals, Proprietary Concerns, Partnership Firms, Private Limited Companies, and other legally recognized entities can apply.

02

Nature of Assistance

Includes Term Loans, Overdraft Limits, Working Capital, and Composite Loans for acquiring capital assets or expanding enterprises.

03

Offerings

Loan up to ₹10 Lakhs at attractive rates — no collateral required. Support for shops, services, auto-rickshaws, tailoring units, and more.

04

Interest Rates

Interest charged up to 18% per annum on reducing balance, depending on borrower profile. Special offers for select applicants.

05

Processing Fees

Up to 2% on loan disbursement, with possible 0% charges for eligible women entrepreneurs under Mahaveer Program.

06

Security

Unsecured business loans—no collateral needed. Guarantees may apply based on profile and financial history.

07

Refinance Scheme

Offered by Mahaveer to last-mile lenders, ensuring affordable interest rates for women entrepreneurs.

08

Tenor

Flexible repayment up to 36 months, aligned to business cash flow and income generation cycles.

OUR WORKING PROCESS

Empowering Women through Structured

Steps for Sustainable Growth

The Mahaveer Program follows a transparent and inclusive model designed to identify, train, and support women entrepreneurs. Our process ensures that every participant receives the tools, funding, and mentorship required for long-term success.

01

Identification & Outreach

We engage with women entrepreneurs, self-help groups, and aspiring business owners to identify promising ideas and connect them with program partners and financial institutions.

02

Capacity Building & Financial Support

Through business training, mentorship, and Mahaveer Program loans, we help women establish strong ventures in trading, manufacturing, and agriculture-linked sectors.

03

Monitoring & Sustainable Impact

We ensure every supported business thrives through continuous monitoring, mentorship, and evaluation—fostering lasting financial independence and community growth.

For more details on women empowerment initiatives, visit our Mahaveer Program Services section.
